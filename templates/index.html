Identifying Greenwashing

We found five major criteria that often identify greenwashing:

  • Lesser of two evils. Consider whether the product category is environmentally destructive.
  • Vague claims. Do you believe what the packaging says?
  • Irrelevance. CFC free? That's banned for all products.
  • Fibbing. Companies have been known to lie.
  • Deceptively green packaging. During interviews, consumers pointed out how simple, green packaging makes an impression.
